   IBM's System I was a series of midrange computer systems. The platform was initially released in June 1988 as the AS/400 (Application System/400), with OS/400 as the operating system. It was renamed the eServer iSeries in 2000. (with the 400 sometimes added[1]). It was renamed System I again in 2006 as part of IBM's rebranding push.
